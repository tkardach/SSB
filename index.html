Saratoga High Sports Boosters

We are a non-profit organization that supports and augments all sports at Saratoga High School. Membership contributions fund annual athletic concussion testing, strength and conditioning training, two annual scholarships, the 8th grade sports night, our on-campus staff/student softball event, and the SHS signing event for students recruited to play sports in college. We also fund athletic capital improvements not paid by the district and we offer season game passes to all members.

Sports Booster Scholarship

Sports Boosters awards a $1000 scholarship to two graduating student athletes each year, one female and one male, who have earned a varsity team letter and maintained a 3.0 GPA. This scholarship is awarded based on academic achievement, community service, and demonstrated leadership.
