Scholarships

Sports Boosters awards a $1000 scholarship to two graduating student-athletes each year, one female and one male, who have earned a varsity team letter and maintained a 3.0 GPA. This scholarship is awarded based on academic achievement, community service, and demonstrated leadership. Download the application form.
