Falcon Sports Sponsorship Program

Every year over 1,000 student athletes participate in one of Saratoga High School’s 24 sports teams such as football, soccer, basketball, volleyball, tennis, cross country, track, softball and baseball. Your donations to the SHS Sports Boosters help to cultivate the next generation of student athletes and future leaders. These donations allow us to ensure that athletes are kept safe with proper equipment, facilities and uniforms. Contributions and donations from our corporate sponsors provide a vital community partnership with our athletic program. We invite you to support our athletic program while also promoting your business to the hundreds of local families that participate in SHS’s athletic programs.

Get involved

PLATINUM FALCON ($5000+)

As a PLATINUM Falcon, you will have a large banner placed on the visitor’s side of our stadium, a large cloth banner (15” by 45”) placed on the gym promotional sponsors banner, and be recognized in our Falcon newspaper. Your logo/link will be featured on our sponsorship page of the Saratoga High School Boosters website as a PLATINUM member. Your generous support will be recognized at athletic awards ceremonies during the school year. Your business will receive a recognition plaque for your support that you can display in your business to let your patrons know of the generous support of our student athletes.
